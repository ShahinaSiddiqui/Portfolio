Counterfeit Currency Identification — Bank Tellers

A specialized e-learning module for bank tellers to identify counterfeit currency with greater accuracy and confidence. The interactive module covers all denominations in circulation in India, aligning with RBI guidance and focusing on security features and red flags.

Scenario-based assessments simulate real-world situations, reinforce key concepts, and support on-the-job skill transfer. The client appreciated the practical relevance, clean design, and immediate applicability for frontline employees.

Ethics Assessment — Vikram & Bhetal Inspired

An engaging ethics training assessment for a leading financial institution that blends the timeless Indian tale of Vikram and Bhetal with modern workplace dilemmas. The ethical compass is woven into the narrative, turning compliance into an immersive experience.

The module earned excellent feedback for originality, cultural resonance, and learner engagement.

Curation to creation: end-to-end learning design

Instructional Designing

I turn raw content into objective-aligned, dev-ready learning paths. Starting with design documents and storyboards, I restructure material into learner-friendly content that’s chunked, sequenced, and written in clear, simple language. Dev-ready specs guide video, e-learning, or LMS builds with measurable objectives mapped to activities, checks, and feedback.

Learning Builds

I build SCORM-ready, LMS-scale e-learning, microlearning, and product/onboarding modules that are interactive, mobile-first, and tailored to the need. Where appropriate, I weave in case studies, scenario flows, and varied assessments to align with—and evidence—the learning objectives.

Game-based Learning

More than rewards and badges: I design learning that works like a real game—objectives map to levels with lock/unlock gates, checkpoints, and boss-style challenges. Content, decision points, feedback, and a game-like UI/UX are co-designed so engagement turns into retention and skill transfer.

Project Journey

Yaksha Prashna — Ethics Challenge

Project Brief

Scenario-based dilemmas inspired by the Mahabharata’s Yaksha Prashna to build principled, practical decision-making.

Conducting Need Analysis

Interviews and case scans; identified recurring value conflicts and policy misconceptions.

Findings from the Needs Analysis

Need to practice trade-offs under pressure with clear reasoning models.

My Expertise in Action

Decision trees, consequence mapping, and feedback framing.

Learning Design Strategy

Prompted reflection, contrastive examples, and retry options for deeper learning.

The Outcome

Better ethical articulation; managers report improved judgment in gray-area tasks.

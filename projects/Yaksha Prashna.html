Project Name: Yaksha Prashna — Ethics Challenge

Project Brief:

Scenario-based dilemmas inspired by Yaksha Prashna.

Conducting Need Analysis:

Interviews and case scans revealed recurring value conflicts.

  • Misconceptions to correct
  • Pressure points
  • Reasoning patterns

Findings from the Needs Analysis:

Practice trade-offs under pressure with clear reasoning models.

Audience :

Corporate staff

Module Duration

30–40 minutes

My Expertise in Action:

  • Decision trees
  • Consequence mapping
  • Feedback framing

Learning Design Strategy:

Prompted reflection, contrastive examples, and retry options.

The Outcome

Better ethical articulation; improved judgment in gray areas.

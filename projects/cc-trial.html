Counterfeit Currency – Teller Training

A specialized e-learning module for bank tellers to confidently detect counterfeit Indian currency. With scenario-based checks and interactive gameplay, the module aligned with RBI guidelines and delivered measurable on-the-job impact.

Brief

The client wanted a game-based module covering all current denominations and highlighting key security features and red flags per RBI standards.

Audience

Frontline bank tellers handling cash transactions daily.

Module Duration

40–50 minutes (self-paced).

Deliverable

Interactive, game-based e-learning with simulations and assessments.

Project Duration

2 months.

Instructional Design eLearning Development Gamification & Learning Strategy

Need Analysis

  • Common errors tellers made in counterfeit detection
  • Denominations posing the greatest risk
  • Realistic time available for learning
  • Compliance requirements tied to RBI guidelines

These insights led to a concise, highly interactive, practice-oriented solution that fits teller workflow.

Learning Design Strategy

The module was designed as a game-based experience inspired by “Money Heist,” where the narrative and mechanics are tied to the learning content. The experience unfolds across four heist levels, each focusing on a denomination, with progressive challenges and timed checks.

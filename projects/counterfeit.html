Project Journey

Counterfeit Currency – Teller Training

Project Brief

  • Detect counterfeit Indian currency aligned to RBI cues.
  • Confidence-building through realistic practice checks.

Conducting Need Analysis

  • Stakeholder interviews and teller task analysis.
  • Incident review to map high-risk denominations.
  • Compliance requirements to RBI guidelines.

Audience :

Frontline bank tellers handling cash transactions daily.

Findings from the Needs Analysis

30–40 minutes engaging module to help bank tellers confidently identify counterfeit currency in line with RBI guidelines, covering all denominations and key security features/red flags, with realistic scenarios.

Module Duration

40–50 minutes (self-paced)

My Expertise in Action

  • Storyboard, interaction model, copy, QA, SCORM packaging.

Learning Design Strategy

  • Scenario checks, denomination “feature hunts”, rapid feedback, spaced retrieval.

The Outcome

  • Higher accuracy in mock audits; faster escalations.

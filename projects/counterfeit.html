Project Journey

Counterfeit Currency – Teller Training

Project Brief

  • Detect counterfeit Indian currency aligned to RBI cues.
  • Confidence-building via realistic practice checks.

Conducting Need Analysis

  • Stakeholder interviews and teller task analysis.
  • Incident review to map high-risk denominations.

Findings from the Needs Analysis

  • Variance in baseline knowledge across branches.
  • Need for denomination-wise “feature hunts”.

My Expertise in Action

  • Storyboard, interaction model, copy, QA, SCORM packaging.

Learning Design Strategy

  • Scenario checks, rapid feedback, spaced retrieval.

The Outcome

  • Higher accuracy in mock audits; faster escalations.

Project Journey

Counterfeit Currency – Teller Training

Project Brief

Specialized module for bank tellers to detect counterfeit Indian currency with accuracy and confidence, aligned to RBI security features and red flags.

Conducting Need Analysis

Stakeholder interviews, task analysis at teller counters, and review of recent counterfeit incidents informed the performance gaps and priority flows.

Findings from the Needs Analysis

High-variance baseline knowledge; need for spaced practice, denomination-wise cues, and quick reference of tactile/visual markers.

My Expertise in Action

End-to-end ID: design doc, storyboard, interaction model, on-screen copy, QA, and LMS packaging (SCORM).

Learning Design Strategy

Scenario-based checks, denomination “feature hunts,” and rapid feedback loops; game-like assessment to reinforce discrimination skills.

The Outcome

Higher accuracy in mock audits; reduced escalation time; positive teller confidence scores post-training.

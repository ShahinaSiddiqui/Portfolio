Project Journey

Ethics Assessment — Vikram Bhetal Inspired

Project Brief

  • Narrative-driven dilemmas grounded in policy and practice.

Conducting Need Analysis

  • Policy scan + incident taxonomy; manager interviews.
  • Frequent gray-area decisions surfaced for practice.

Audience :

Corporate staff and managers navigating ethical dilemmas.

Findings from the Needs Analysis

Desire for contextual, non-preachy practice with short cycles and transparent rationales for acceptable choices.

Module Duration

30–40 minutes (self-paced)

My Expertise in Action

  • Story weaving, decision trees, feedback grammar, scoring model, production QA.

Learning Design Strategy

  • Branching narratives with “why it matters” nudges and immediate, doctrine-anchored feedback.

The Outcome

  • Higher completion and discussion rates; stronger ethical reasoning.

Project Journey

Ethics Assessment — Vikram Bhetal Inspired

Project Brief

Narrative-driven ethics assessment blending the Vikram–Bhetal tale with modern workplace dilemmas and reflective feedback.

Conducting Need Analysis

Policy scan, incident taxonomy, and manager interviews to surface frequent gray-area decisions.

Findings from the Needs Analysis

Desire for contextual, non-preachy practice with short cycles and transparent rationales for acceptable choices.

My Expertise in Action

Story weaving, decision trees, feedback grammar, scoring model, and production QA.

Learning Design Strategy

Branching narratives with “why it matters” nudges and immediate, doctrine-anchored feedback.

The Outcome

Higher completion and discussion rates; stronger ethical reasoning in pressure scenarios.

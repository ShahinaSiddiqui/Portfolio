Project Journey

3-in-1 Trading Account Simulation

Project Brief

Guided sandbox for opening/using a 3-in-1 trading account; reduces errors via stepwise hints and guardrails.

Conducting Need Analysis

Process mapping and UI friction logs to pinpoint moments where learners fail or stall.

Findings from the Needs Analysis

High first-time errors; need for micro-steps, validation messages, and “see-then-do” practice.

My Expertise in Action

Interaction flow, copy, assessment logic, pilot feedback, and iteration.

Learning Design Strategy

Task decomposition, progressive disclosure, and scenario-based error recovery to build automaticity.

The Outcome

Reduced onboarding time; measurable drop in support tickets for first-time users.

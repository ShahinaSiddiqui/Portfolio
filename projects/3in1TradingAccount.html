Project Name: 3-in-1 Trading Account

Project Brief:

The client wanted a guided sandbox to reduce first-time errors and ramp proficiency.

Conducting Need Analysis:

Exploration included process maps, UI friction logs, and support call drivers.

  • Where users restarted flows
  • Validation messages commonly missed
  • How much time is available for practice

Findings from the Needs Analysis:

Need for micro-steps, progressive disclosure, and see-then-do practice.

Audience :

Customer-facing & ops staff

Module Duration

35–45 minutes (self-paced)

My Expertise in Action:

  • Interaction flow
  • Copy
  • Assessment logic
  • Pilot QA

Learning Design Strategy:

Task decomposition, progressive disclosure, and error recovery.

The Outcome

Reduced onboarding time and support tickets.

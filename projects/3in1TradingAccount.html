Project Journey

3-in-1 Trading Account Simulation

Project Brief

  • Guided sandbox to reduce first-time errors and speed proficiency.

Conducting Need Analysis

  • Process mapping and UI friction logs; call-driver analysis.

Audience :

Customer-facing and ops teams opening/servicing 3-in-1 accounts.

Findings from the Needs Analysis

Need for micro-steps, validation messages, and see-then-do practice; reduce restart loops and support tickets.

Module Duration

35–45 minutes (self-paced)

My Expertise in Action

  • Interaction flow, copy, assessment logic, pilot feedback loop.

Learning Design Strategy

  • Task decomposition, progressive disclosure, error recovery.

The Outcome

  • Reduced onboarding time and support tickets.
